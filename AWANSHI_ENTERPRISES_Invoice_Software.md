- Client name, email, phone, GST, address
- Invoice number (auto-increments)

- Discount percentage

### 3. All Invoices

- Toggle invoice status (Pending → Paid → Draft)
- Delete invoices

## How to Create an Invoice

1. Click **New Invoice** tab
2. Enter client details (name, email, phone, address, GST)
3. Set invoice number, invoice date, and due date
4. Add services/items with quantity and rate
5. Adjust GST % and discount % if needed
6. Add payment notes (bank details are pre-filled)
7. Click **Preview** to review the invoice
8. Click **Save Invoice** to save it to the list
9. Use **Print / Save PDF** to download
